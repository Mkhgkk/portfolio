About Bugipay

Bugipay is a mobile payment app that enables users to make transfers to other users on the platform as well as to chat privately and in groups. Bugipay has widely incorporated the use of QR code throughout the app to perform tasks such as making payment, adding friends, and joining groups. Bugipay is aiming to give financial control at low costs to millions of people living in Tanzania and potentially other developing nations through state of the art technologies used by the likes of AliPay and WechatPay in China.
